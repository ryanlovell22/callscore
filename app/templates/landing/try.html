Frequently Asked Questions

Is this just a wrapper around Twilio?

No. Twilio (or CallRail) handles your phone numbers and recordings. CallOutcome is the AI pipeline on top — it transcribes every call with Whisper, classifies the outcome with GPT, and gives you a dashboard with partner-shareable proof. The integration is just where the audio comes from.

Do I need to change my call tracking setup?

No. Connect your existing Twilio or CallRail account during onboarding. Your tracking numbers stay the same — CallOutcome reads your recordings, it doesn't replace your phone system.

What happens after the 50 free calls?

You upgrade to Starter ($29/mo for 100 calls) or stop. No surprise charges, no automatic upgrades. You're on the free plan until you choose to change it.

How is this different from CallRail CI?

CallRail's Conversation Intelligence costs $100+/month extra and is built for marketing teams with keyword spotting. CallOutcome answers one question: did this call book a job? It also gives you shared dashboards for partners — something CallRail doesn't offer. From $29/month.

Can I try it with my real calls before committing?

Yes. The free plan processes 50 real calls from your Twilio or CallRail account — full transcripts, AI classification, and dashboard. No demo data, no sandbox.

Is my Twilio/CallRail API key safe?

Credentials are encrypted at rest, all data is isolated per account, and we never share your call recordings or transcripts with third parties.

Who built this?

A rank-and-rent operator in Australia running 20+ lead gen sites. Built to solve my own problem — I needed to know which calls booked jobs without listening to every recording. Now it does it for others too.
